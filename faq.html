Frequently Asked Questions

Clarity, transparency, and soulful support every step of the way.

📝 What kind of books do you publish?

We specialize in purpose-driven books — including memoirs, self-help, spiritual teachings, personal development, and visionary fiction. If your story is soul-aligned and meant to serve others, we’d love to help you publish it.

💸 How much does publishing with Divinius cost?

We offer flexible, affordable packages designed for indie authors. Prices vary based on how hands-on or hands-off you want the process to be. Visit our packages page to learn more.

⏳ How long does the process take?

Typical timelines range from 1–4 months depending on the level of editing, design, and publishing prep needed. We’ll work with your desired timeline and keep you updated along the way.

📣 Can I talk to someone before signing up?

Absolutely! You can schedule a free discovery call and we'll answer any questions, walk you through the process, and feel into whether it’s the right fit for you.
